About Us

Leading Green Building Consultancy in Rwanda

Sanifu Limited is an architectural and green building consultancy firm operating in Rwanda for over a decade. We partner with professional design teams to deliver sustainable, environmentally-friendly building solutions.

Our mission is to promote sustainability in architecture and construction through innovative green building practices, EDGE certification, and comprehensive sustainable design services.
